App Containers

App Container – Default layout

We deal with displaying a lot of data in our main section pages like orders, products and customers. These tend to be either in tabular or grid format. To utilise the space we have we provide a full-width layout by default, in which you can use a fluid, 12 column grid.

Notes:

Wrap the content of your Micro-App inside this container.

App Container – Fixed layout

In a lot of other cases, Control Panel pages are form based, or other text and image content. These types of pages don't suit a full width layout, and instead have a maximum width of 1200px, to reduce reading line length.

These pages can often have form actions, fixed to the bottom of the screen too.

Notes:

As Micro-Apps live inside an iframe, we do not consider the left hand side bar as part of the 1000px maximum width.
