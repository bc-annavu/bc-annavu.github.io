Z-Index

All BigCommerce components should live on the predefined scale for the z axis. This keeps our component visual depth consistent and predictable across the entire UI.

Token Value
zIndex("lowest") 5
zIndex("lower") 10
zIndex("low") 20
zIndex("high") 50
zIndex("higher") 100
zIndex("highest") 500
