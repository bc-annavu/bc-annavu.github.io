Notes:

The Page Message is a simple pattern consisting of a Panel and the pageMessage component. The content within the component is aligned to the center, and the pattern as a whole is intended to be the primary content holder for the view it's contained in.

The pageMessage should live inside the panel-body, and cannot be dismissed or avoided. The panel also has no panel header.

Common uses for page messaging include Page Statuses or Upsell Pages.
